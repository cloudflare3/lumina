About Lumina

Lumina is derived from Latin, meaning "light." At Lumina Dental Clinic, we chose this name to reflect our mission: to provide radiant smiles and world-class care that you deserve.

Who We Are

Lumina is a comprehensive dental entity that merges art and expertise in the field of dental care and aesthetics. We proudly stay updated with the latest international dental technologies and treatment approaches.

Our in-house laboratory is equipped with cutting-edge digital systems, from intraoral scanning for precise measurements to advanced smile design software and 3D printing machines. We use the finest internationally certified materials, all supervised by a talented team of dentists and technicians with decades of experience—true artists in their field.

At Lumina, we offer state-of-the-art medical equipment and strictly follow top-tier sterilization and hygiene protocols, aligning with global dental standards.

Our Process

Your treatment journey begins with an initial remote diagnosis through panoramic X-rays, followed by an in-clinic visit where we tailor a personalized treatment plan. We provide a clear estimate of the cost and timeline.

Throughout the process, our medical team remains in contact with you during and after treatment to ensure successful results and your complete satisfaction. Our integrated staff schedules all your appointments efficiently with doctors and departments, making your visit smooth and stress-free.
